Unlock liquidity and efficiency.

Quant Flow is a ready-to-deploy, API-driven programmable digital money platform built with our Overledger technology. It enables banks, fintechs and PSPs to innovate and transform their current infrastructure with account automation and payment programmability that will shape the next generation of digital finance.

Combining programmable payments logic, rapid development of new products and seamless API integration, Quant Flow can future-proof existing infrastructure, offer industry-leading customer experiences and unlock long-term returns.

Realising the potential of programmable payments

Intelligent payment orchestration

  • Automate complex payment scenarios with PayScript, a domain-specific language for programmable money
  • Pre-built payment templates for tax automation, payroll and treasury management
  • Trigger-based architecture links bank accounts to real-world events

Secure & compliant digital money infrastructure

  • Regulatory-grade security with integration to compliance tools
  • Fraud prevention & risk analytics feeds for real-time transaction monitoring
  • Smart financial controls for liquidity, settlements and fund locks

Interoperability across banking & digital assets

  • Seamless connectivity direct to banking systems or via Open Banking
  • Stablecoin and tokenised deposit support for cross-border and institutional payments
  • Integration requires minimal or no change to existing systems
